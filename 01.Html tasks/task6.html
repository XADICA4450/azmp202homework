About Me

Mauro Emanuel Icardi is an Argentinean professional football player who presently plays as a striker for the Argentinean national football team and for the Italian football club ‘Internazionale.’ He has been serving as the captain of ‘Internazionale’ since 2015. He started his career in football with the Spanish football team ‘Vecindario’ at the age of 9 and proved his mettle by netting more than 500 goals in various youth categories of the club.
